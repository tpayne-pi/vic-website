Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join?

Visit our TigerLink page and click "Join." It's free and open to all LSU students.

Do I need investing experience?

Not at all. We welcome everyone from complete beginners to experienced traders. Our meetings are designed to be educational at all levels.

When and where do you meet?

Meeting times and locations are posted on our TigerLink page and announced to members. Check TigerLink for the latest schedule.

Can I present a stock pitch?

Yes! We encourage all members to present investment ideas. Reach out to our leadership team if you'd like to present at an upcoming meeting.
